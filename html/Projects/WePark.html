3D Website.

GOAL
  • Find parking faster and cheaper in dense areas.
  • Unify short-term and long-term parking in one place with reliable info.
  • Enable transparent choices via community input and seamless pay/navigation.
OVERVIEW
  • Mobile app that aggregates public & private spots and lets users compare by price, distance, availability on a map.
  • Marketplace listings for owners (post spaces) and renters (filters for lease length, price, distance, type).
  • Integrated payments and navigation handoff (e.g., open route in Google Maps).
HIGHLIGHTS
  • Competitive scan (GreenP, Kijiji, Passport) → gaps in real-time info, UX, and regional coverage shaped the MVP.
  • Two core flows: Driver (search → compare → navigate/pay) and Owner (list → chat → lease).
  • IA & screens: Search, Listing, Detail, Chat, Checkout—kept intentionally light for quick decisions.
