Wiki Redesign.

GOAL
  • Scan faster, read easier on desktop & mobile.
  • Find sections quickly with a clearer table of contents.
  • Keep the Wikipedia look, but reduce clutter.
OVERVIEW
  • Infobox-first layout for key facts; sticky/slide-in TOC (desktop left, mobile drawer).
  • Popular Discussion cards surface Talk-page highlights without leaving the article.
  • Simplified header with global search and consistent actions.
HIGHLIGHTS
  • Clear hierarchy: bigger headings, breathable spacing, improved contrast.
  • TOC redesign: anchor links + scrollspy; collapsible in mobile drawer.
  • Card modules: “Popular Discussion” and media blocks for quick context.
  • Brand-respectful minimalism: classic Wikipedia styling, fewer dividers, more whitespace.
