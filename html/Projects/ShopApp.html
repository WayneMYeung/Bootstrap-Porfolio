ShopApp.

GOAL
  • Browse → pick → buy in as few taps as possible.
  • Give clear sizing & pricing to cut hesitation/returns.
  • Keep checkout fast and predictable on mobile.
OVERVIEW
  • Mobile shopping flow prototyped in Adobe XD (iPhone frames).
  • Home → grid → product → cart → checkout with search, sort, and filters.
  • Cart & checkout: promo, shipping, address, payment, order review.
HIGHLIGHTS
  • Bottom nav for Home, Search, Cart, Account; sticky Add to Cart on PDP.
  • Card layout with sale/new badges, star ratings, quick wishlist.
  • Stepwise checkout (Cart → Address → Payment → Review) for clarity.
  • Reusable design components (cards, chips, buttons) for consistent UI and fast edits.
